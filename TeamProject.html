About Us

Trois Pour Trois was founded in 2024 by Tayler Mitchell,Cameron Murphy & Ashanti Thomas. The name came from the bond of the owners and their dedication to make sure that each other succeeds in life. Their goal was to create a place that makes you forget everyday struggles but still be able to let loose and have fun.

Located in Saint-Tropez, this hotel is a primary beach front hotel with the luxury feel of high class france. With its own private beach & beach club, this hotel is the perfect getway from everyday life.
